# Sales & Customer Success

| # | Role | Description | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | Revenue Operations Manager (RevOps) | Aligns sales, marketing and customer success through data and processes | CRM optimization, pipeline analytics, process automation, forecasting | CTO / CMO Digital |
| 2 | Sales Engineer / Pre-Sales Consultant | Supports the sales cycle with technical expertise and demonstrations | Technical demos, POCs, solution design, RFP responses, objection handling | Sales Operations Manager / CTO |
| 3 | Customer Success Manager | Ensures customers get value from the product and maximizes satisfaction | Onboarding, health scores, churn prevention, QBRs, upsell identification | Revenue Operations Manager |
| 4 | Inside Sales Specialist | Sells tech products and services remotely through digital channels | Cold outreach, qualification, CRM, pipeline management, closing | Sales Operations Manager |
| 5 | Sales Operations Manager | Optimizes sales team processes and tools to increase efficiency | Salesforce admin, sales analytics, territory planning, compensation | Revenue Operations Manager |
| 6 | CRM Specialist | Configures and manages the CRM platform to support the sales team | Salesforce, HubSpot, workflows, custom objects, reporting, integrations | Sales Operations Manager / IT Director |
| 7 | Account Executive (Tech) | Manages the complete sales cycle for technology products and services | Prospecting, discovery calls, negotiation, contract closing, forecasting | Sales Operations Manager |
| 8 | Customer Onboarding Specialist | Guides new customers through product setup and adoption | Onboarding playbooks, training, setup guides, time-to-value optimization | Customer Success Manager |
| 9 | Renewal Manager | Manages contract renewals and prevents customer churn | Renewal forecasting, risk assessment, negotiation, retention strategies | Customer Success Manager |
| 10 | Sales Enablement Specialist | Creates materials and training to help the sales team close more deals | Battlecards, sales playbooks, training programs, competitive analysis | Sales Operations Manager / Revenue Operations Manager |
