# Sales & Customer Success

| # | Role | Description | What They Do | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|--------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | Revenue Operations Manager (RevOps) | Aligns sales, marketing and customer success through data, processes and unified revenue strategy | Pipeline analytics and forecasting, sales process automation, territory and compensation planning, CRM optimization, cross-functional revenue alignment | CRM optimization, pipeline analytics, process automation, forecasting, sales analytics, territory planning | CTO / CMO Digital |
| 2 | Sales Engineer / Pre-Sales Consultant | Supports the sales cycle with technical expertise and demonstrations | Technical product demos and POCs, solution architecture for prospects, RFP and technical proposal responses, objection handling with technical depth | Technical demos, POCs, solution design, RFP responses, objection handling | Sales Operations Manager / CTO |
| 3 | Customer Success Manager | Ensures customers get value from the product across the full lifecycle from onboarding to renewal | Customer onboarding and adoption playbooks, health score monitoring and churn prevention, QBRs and upsell identification, renewal forecasting and contract negotiation, time-to-value optimization | Onboarding, health scores, churn prevention, QBRs, upsell identification, renewal forecasting, retention strategies | Revenue Operations Manager |
| 4 | Inside Sales Specialist | Sells tech products and services remotely through digital channels | Cold outreach and lead qualification, pipeline management and deal tracking, product demos via video calls, proposal creation and contract closing | Cold outreach, qualification, CRM, pipeline management, closing | Revenue Operations Manager |
| 5 | CRM Specialist | Configures and manages the CRM platform to support the sales team | CRM workflow and automation configuration, custom objects and data model design, sales reporting and dashboard creation, third-party integrations and data hygiene | Salesforce, HubSpot, workflows, custom objects, reporting, integrations | Revenue Operations Manager / IT Director |
| 6 | Account Executive (Tech) | Manages the complete sales cycle for technology products and services | Prospecting and discovery calls, solution presentations and negotiations, contract closing and deal forecasting, strategic account planning and expansion | Prospecting, discovery calls, negotiation, contract closing, forecasting | Revenue Operations Manager |
| 7 | Sales Enablement Specialist | Creates materials and training to help the sales team close more deals | Battlecards and competitive analysis documents, sales playbook creation and maintenance, training programs and certification paths, win/loss analysis and messaging refinement | Battlecards, sales playbooks, training programs, competitive analysis | Revenue Operations Manager |
