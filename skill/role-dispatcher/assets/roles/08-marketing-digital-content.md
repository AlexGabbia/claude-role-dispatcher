# Marketing & Digital Content

| # | Role | Description | What They Do | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|--------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) Digital | Executive responsible for the company's digital marketing strategy | Defines the overall digital marketing vision and roadmap, allocates budgets across channels and campaigns, leads and mentors the marketing team, reports marketing KPIs to the board | Marketing strategy, brand positioning, budget allocation, team leadership, stakeholder communication | CEO / Board |
| 2 | Digital Marketing Specialist | Manages online marketing campaigns across multiple digital channels including paid search | Builds and optimizes Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns, runs A/B tests on ad creatives and landing pages, manages keyword bidding and quality scores, tracks campaign performance dashboards | Google Ads, Meta Ads, Bing Ads, campaign management, A/B testing, keyword bidding, quality score, analytics | Performance Marketing Manager / CMO Digital |
| 3 | Performance Marketing Manager | Manages performance campaigns focused on metrics like CPA and ROAS including programmatic and affiliate channels | Oversees paid media budget allocation and bid strategies, manages programmatic advertising via DSP and RTB platforms, runs affiliate programs and partner recruitment, builds attribution models to measure true channel value | Paid media, attribution modeling, bid optimization, ROAS, CPA, DSP, RTB, affiliate platforms, commission structures, partner tracking | CMO Digital |
| 4 | SEO Specialist | Optimizes websites to improve search engine rankings and organic visibility | Conducts keyword research and competitive gap analysis, performs technical SEO audits and fixes crawl issues, develops link building strategies and outreach campaigns, monitors rankings and organic traffic in GSC and analytics tools | On-page SEO, technical SEO, keyword research, link building, GSC, crawl analysis | Performance Marketing Manager / CMO Digital |
| 5 | Content Strategist | Defines editorial strategy and manages the full lifecycle of digital content | Builds and maintains the editorial calendar across all channels, creates editorial guidelines and governance frameworks, plans and publishes content via CMS platforms, audits existing content for gaps and optimization opportunities | Content calendar, editorial guidelines, audience analysis, content audit, CMS, content scheduling, editorial workflow | CMO Digital |
| 6 | Digital Brand Manager | Manages brand identity and perception online including influencer partnerships | Develops and enforces brand guidelines and tone of voice, monitors brand sentiment and online reputation, identifies and negotiates collaborations with influencers and creators, tracks influencer campaign ROI and brand lift | Brand guidelines, tone of voice, brand monitoring, positioning, influencer outreach, contract negotiation, campaign tracking | CMO Digital |
| 7 | Marketing Data Analyst | Analyzes marketing campaign data and web traffic to optimize performance | Builds dashboards tracking campaign KPIs and attribution, analyzes user behavior with heatmaps and funnel reports, runs cohort analyses to measure retention and LTV, manages analytics tag implementation and data quality | Google Analytics, GA4, Mixpanel, SQL, dashboard building, attribution, heatmaps, Hotjar, funnel analysis, cohort analysis, tag management | Performance Marketing Manager / CMO Digital |
| 8 | Growth Hacker | Uses creative and data-driven techniques to accelerate product growth including conversion optimization | Designs and runs rapid experimentation sprints, builds viral loops and referral programs, optimizes conversion rates through A/B tests on funnels and landing pages, analyzes heatmaps and user flows to remove friction points | Viral loops, referral programs, experimentation, product-led growth, A/B testing, funnel optimization, heatmaps, landing page design | CMO Digital / Product Manager |
| 9 | Social Media Manager | Manages social media presence, community engagement and user-generated content | Creates and schedules social media content across platforms, moderates and animates online communities, designs UGC campaigns and curates user content, tracks engagement metrics and social analytics | Content creation, scheduling tools, community engagement, analytics, community platforms, moderation, UGC campaigns, content curation, rights management | Content Strategist / CMO Digital |
| 10 | Marketing Automation Specialist | Configures and optimizes marketing automation platforms including email campaigns | Designs automated workflows for lead nurturing and onboarding, manages email campaign creation and deliverability, builds lead scoring models and audience segmentation, monitors drip campaign performance and conversion rates | HubSpot, Marketo, Mailchimp, SendGrid, workflows, lead scoring, segmentation, drip campaigns, deliverability | Performance Marketing Manager / CMO Digital |
| 11 | Digital PR Specialist | Manages digital public relations and online reputation | Writes and distributes press releases to digital media outlets, conducts media outreach and builds journalist relationships, handles crisis communication and reputation management, monitors media coverage and sentiment | Press releases, media outreach, crisis communication, online reputation, media monitoring | Digital Brand Manager / CMO Digital |
| 12 | Digital Copywriter | Writes persuasive copy for websites, landing pages and digital campaigns | Crafts landing page and ad copy optimized for conversion, writes SEO-friendly blog posts and web content, develops UX microcopy for digital products, runs A/B tests on headlines and CTAs | Copywriting, UX writing, SEO writing, A/B testing, tone of voice | Content Strategist / CMO Digital |
| 13 | Digital Content Producer | Creates and produces video, audio and multimedia content for digital platforms | Produces and edits video content for social media and YouTube, manages podcast production from recording to distribution, optimizes thumbnails and metadata for platform algorithms, handles audio editing and show notes for streaming platforms | Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, YouTube optimization, thumbnails, audio editing, Audacity, podcast distribution, show notes, guest management | Content Strategist / Social Media Manager |
