# Design & User Experience

| # | Role | Description | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | Product Designer | Combines UX and UI to design digital products from concept to launch | Figma, user research, prototyping, design thinking, handoff | Product Manager / VP of Engineering |
| 2 | UX Designer | Designs user experience, studying how people interact with the product | Wireframing, user flows, personas, usability testing, Figma | Product Designer / Product Manager |
| 3 | UI Designer | Designs the visual appearance of interfaces: colors, typography, layout | Visual design, color theory, typography, design systems, Figma | Product Designer / Product Manager |
| 4 | UX Researcher | Conducts user research to understand needs, behaviors and problems | Interviews, surveys, A/B testing, analytics, personas, usability labs | Product Designer / Product Manager |
| 5 | Web Designer | Creates graphic design and aesthetic appearance of websites | HTML/CSS basics, responsive design, Adobe XD, landing pages, visual hierarchy | Product Designer / Product Manager |
| 6 | Information Architect | Organizes and structures content in a logical and navigable way | Sitemaps, card sorting, taxonomy, content modeling, navigation patterns | Product Designer / UX Designer |
| 7 | Interaction Designer | Designs animations and micro-interactions between user and interface | Motion design, state machines, prototyping, animation, transitions | Product Designer / UX Designer |
| 8 | Design System Lead | Creates and maintains reusable component libraries to ensure visual consistency | Storybook, design tokens, component APIs, Figma libraries, documentation | Product Designer / VP of Engineering |
| 9 | Service Designer | Designs the entire service experience, including digital and physical touchpoints | Service blueprints, journey maps, stakeholder mapping, workshops | Product Manager / Product Designer |
| 10 | Accessibility Specialist | Ensures digital products are usable by people with disabilities | WCAG, screen readers, ARIA, inclusive design, color contrast, audits | Product Designer / UX Designer |
| 11 | Motion Designer | Creates animations and transitions for digital interfaces and multimedia content | After Effects, Lottie, CSS animations, micro-interactions, video | Product Designer / UI Designer |
