# IT Support & Administration

| # | Role | Description | What They Do | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|--------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | IT Service Manager | Manages IT service delivery following frameworks like ITIL | Defines and monitors SLAs, manages the service catalog, runs incident/problem review meetings, drives continual service improvement, oversees ITSM tool configuration | ITIL, SLA management, incident management, service catalog, ITSM tools | IT Director / CIO |
| 2 | IT Coordinator | Coordinates daily IT activities and liaises with other departments | Schedules IT tasks and resources across teams, tracks project milestones and deliverables, produces operational status reports, manages vendor communications | Cross-team coordination, scheduling, reporting, vendor liaison | IT Service Manager / IT Director |
| 3 | IT Support Specialist | Resolves hardware and software technical issues for enterprise users across all support tiers and locations | Triages and resolves tickets from L1 to L3, performs workstation setup and imaging, provides on-site and remote troubleshooting, manages peripheral and printer configurations, handles password resets and software installations | Troubleshooting, ticketing systems, remote support, hardware repair, Windows/macOS setup, imaging, on-site support, cabling | IT Service Manager / IT Coordinator |
| 4 | IT Auditor & Compliance | Verifies IT system compliance with regulations and corporate policies, and ensures ongoing adherence to applicable laws | Conducts internal IT audits and control testing, performs compliance gap analysis, writes and maintains IT policies, prepares audit evidence packages, monitors regulatory changes (GDPR, HIPAA, SOX) | Audit frameworks, ISO 27001, SOX, risk assessment, control testing, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, policy writing, compliance monitoring | IT Director / CISO |
| 5 | IT Asset Manager | Tracks and manages the full inventory of corporate IT resources including hardware, software licenses and contracts | Maintains the CMDB and asset registry, manages software license compliance and renewals, tracks asset lifecycle and depreciation, prepares license audit documentation, optimizes IT asset costs | CMDB, asset lifecycle, depreciation, inventory tools, procurement, license tracking, SAM tools, audit preparation, renewal management | IT Service Manager / IT Director |
| 6 | IT Procurement Specialist | Manages purchases of hardware, software and IT services | Drafts RFPs/RFQs and evaluates vendor proposals, negotiates contracts and pricing, tracks procurement budgets, manages purchase orders and delivery timelines | RFP/RFQ, vendor evaluation, contract negotiation, budget tracking | IT Director / IT Asset Manager |
| 7 | NOC Technician | Monitors networks and systems from the Network Operations Center | Watches real-time dashboards and monitoring tools, triages alerts and classifies severity, executes escalation procedures, documents incidents during shift handovers | Monitoring tools, alert triage, escalation procedures, shift operations | IT Operations Manager / Network Engineer |
