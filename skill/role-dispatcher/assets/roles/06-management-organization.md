# Management & Organization

| # | Role | Description | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | CTO (Chief Technology Officer) | Executive responsible for the company's technology strategy | Technology vision, architecture decisions, team building, budgeting | CEO / Board |
| 2 | CIO (Chief Information Officer) | Executive responsible for information systems and digital transformation | IT strategy, digital transformation, governance, vendor management | CEO / Board |
| 3 | VP of Engineering | Leads the entire software engineering organization | Organizational design, hiring, scaling teams, process optimization | CTO |
| 4 | IT Director | Directs the IT department and defines technology priorities | IT strategy, budget planning, vendor management, governance | CIO / CTO |
| 5 | CDO (Chief Data Officer) | Executive responsible for enterprise data strategy and governance | Data strategy, data governance, analytics vision, compliance | CEO / Board |
| 6 | Product Manager | Defines product vision and guides development based on user needs | User stories, roadmapping, stakeholder management, metrics, discovery | VP of Engineering / CTO |
| 7 | Engineering Manager | Manages the development team, combining technical leadership and people management | 1:1s, sprint planning, performance reviews, hiring, mentoring | VP of Engineering |
| 8 | Project Manager (PM) | Plans, coordinates and oversees IT projects within time and budget | Gantt charts, budgets, timelines, risk registers, stakeholder comms | IT Director / Delivery Manager |
| 9 | Program Manager | Coordinates multiple related projects to achieve strategic objectives | Program roadmaps, risk management, dependency tracking, reporting | IT Director / VP of Engineering |
| 10 | Tech Lead | Guides team technical decisions and serves as architecture reference | Code reviews, architecture decisions, technical debt, mentoring | Engineering Manager |
| 11 | Product Owner | Manages the product backlog and priorities within the Agile framework | Backlog grooming, user stories, acceptance criteria, sprint planning | Product Manager |
| 12 | Business Analyst | Analyzes business processes and translates requirements into technical specifications | Requirements gathering, process mapping, BRDs, gap analysis, BPMN | Project Manager / Product Manager |
| 13 | Delivery Manager | Ensures projects are delivered with quality and on schedule | Release planning, capacity management, flow metrics, impediment removal | IT Director / VP of Engineering |
| 14 | Technical Program Manager | Manages complex programs with strong technical components and cross-team dependencies | Cross-team coordination, technical specs, risk management, roadmaps | VP of Engineering / CTO |
| 15 | Scrum Master | Facilitates the Scrum process, removing obstacles and promoting Agile practices | Sprint ceremonies, burndown charts, team facilitation, impediment removal | Engineering Manager / Delivery Manager |
| 16 | Agile Coach | Guides the organization in adopting and improving Agile methodologies | SAFe, LeSS, team coaching, organizational change, metrics, workshops | VP of Engineering / CTO |
| 17 | Change Manager | Manages organizational change related to new technology adoption | Change frameworks, communication plans, stakeholder alignment, training | CIO / IT Director |
