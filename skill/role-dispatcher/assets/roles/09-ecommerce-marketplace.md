# E-commerce & Marketplace

| # | Role | Description | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | Marketplace Manager | Manages company presence on marketplaces like Amazon, eBay and Etsy | Amazon Seller Central, marketplace strategy, listing optimization | E-commerce Director |
| 2 | E-commerce Director | Directs the overall e-commerce strategy of the company | E-commerce strategy, P&L management, platform selection, growth | CMO Digital / CEO |
| 3 | Marketplace Account Manager | Manages seller accounts on marketplaces, optimizing sales and visibility | Account health, Buy Box optimization, seller metrics, compliance | Marketplace Manager |
| 4 | E-commerce Product Manager | Manages the online product catalog and coordinates its evolution | Product roadmap, catalog management, UX optimization, A/B testing | E-commerce Director |
| 5 | Catalog Manager | Curates product listings, pricing and categories on sales platforms | PIM systems, product taxonomy, data quality, bulk editing, SEO | E-commerce Product Manager / E-commerce Director |
| 6 | Marketplace Operations Specialist | Manages logistics, orders and operational performance on marketplaces | Order fulfillment, FBA, inventory sync, returns management, SLAs | Marketplace Manager |
| 7 | E-commerce Analyst | Analyzes sales, conversions and e-commerce KPIs to guide decisions | Google Analytics, conversion funnels, cohort analysis, revenue attribution | E-commerce Director / E-commerce Product Manager |
| 8 | Dropshipping Manager | Manages the dropshipping model (selling without own inventory) | Supplier management, margin optimization, logistics coordination | E-commerce Director / Marketplace Manager |
| 9 | Marketplace Advertising Specialist | Manages advertising campaigns within marketplaces (Amazon Ads, etc.) | Amazon PPC, Sponsored Products, ACoS optimization, keyword targeting | Marketplace Manager / Performance Marketing Manager |
| 10 | Pricing Analyst / Dynamic Pricing Specialist | Optimizes prices in real-time with algorithms and market analysis | Pricing algorithms, competitor monitoring, elasticity analysis, A/B testing | E-commerce Director / E-commerce Product Manager |
