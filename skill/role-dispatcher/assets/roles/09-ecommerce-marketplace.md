# E-commerce & Marketplace

| # | Role | Description | What They Do | Key Skills | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|--------------|------------|------------|
| 1 | E-commerce Director | Directs the overall e-commerce strategy, P&L and daily operations of the company | Defines e-commerce roadmap and channel strategy, owns P&L and revenue targets, selects and governs e-commerce platforms, coordinates cross-functional teams (marketing, logistics, product), reports performance to C-level | E-commerce strategy, P&L management, platform selection, omnichannel growth, team leadership | CMO Digital / CEO |
| 2 | Marketplace Manager | Manages company presence on marketplaces like Amazon, eBay and Etsy, including seller accounts and dropshipping channels | Develops marketplace go-to-market strategy per channel, optimizes account health and Buy Box positioning, manages seller compliance and performance metrics, coordinates dropshipping suppliers and fulfillment partners, negotiates marketplace fees and terms | Amazon Seller Central, marketplace strategy, listing optimization, account health, supplier management, margin optimization | E-commerce Director |
| 3 | E-commerce Product Manager | Manages the online product catalog, its taxonomy and lifecycle evolution | Owns product roadmap and feature prioritization, curates catalog taxonomy and product data quality, runs A/B tests on product pages and UX flows, manages PIM systems and bulk enrichment workflows, aligns catalog strategy with SEO and merchandising | Product roadmap, catalog management, PIM systems, UX optimization, A/B testing, product taxonomy, data quality | E-commerce Director |
| 4 | Marketplace Operations Specialist | Manages logistics, orders and operational performance on marketplaces | Monitors and fulfills orders across multiple marketplaces, manages FBA inventory levels and replenishment, handles returns and customer escalations, syncs inventory across channels to prevent overselling, tracks SLA compliance and operational KPIs | Order fulfillment, FBA, inventory sync, returns management, SLA tracking | Marketplace Manager |
| 5 | E-commerce Analyst | Analyzes sales, conversions and e-commerce KPIs to guide business decisions | Builds conversion funnel dashboards and revenue attribution models, performs cohort and customer lifetime value analysis, identifies drop-off points and recommends UX improvements, forecasts sales trends and seasonal demand, delivers weekly performance reports to leadership | Google Analytics, conversion funnels, cohort analysis, revenue attribution, data visualization | E-commerce Director / E-commerce Product Manager |
| 6 | Marketplace Advertising Specialist | Manages advertising campaigns within marketplaces (Amazon Ads, Walmart Connect, etc.) | Plans and launches Sponsored Products and Sponsored Brands campaigns, optimizes ACoS and ROAS through bid and keyword management, conducts keyword research and negative keyword pruning, runs A/B tests on ad creatives and placements, reports campaign performance and budget utilization | Amazon PPC, Sponsored Products, ACoS optimization, keyword targeting, campaign analytics | Marketplace Manager / Performance Marketing Manager |
| 7 | Pricing Analyst | Optimizes prices in real-time with algorithms, competitive intelligence and elasticity models | Designs and tunes dynamic pricing algorithms, monitors competitor pricing across channels, runs price elasticity and margin impact analyses, configures automated repricing rules and guardrails, delivers pricing recommendations for promotions and launches | Pricing algorithms, competitor monitoring, elasticity analysis, A/B testing, repricing tools | E-commerce Director / E-commerce Product Manager |
